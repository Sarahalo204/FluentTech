### Q1: Tell me about yourself.
**Why interviewers ask this:** They want a concise professional summary that shows your fit for the role. 
**Target CEFR level:** B2
**Model Answer (STAR format where applicable):** I am a software engineer with three years of experience in fintech product teams. In my last role, I worked on a mobile dashboard project that improved customer reporting. I contributed to UI improvements and backend integration, collaborating with designers and QA. The result was a 20% increase in customer satisfaction and faster release cycles. I enjoy working in agile teams and I am looking for a company where I can use my communication skills and technical experience to support product growth.
**Key phrases to use:** "I am a software engineer", "I worked on", "I contributed to", "the result was", "I enjoy working in agile teams"
**❌ What Saudi graduates often say instead:** I am from Riyadh, I studied computer science, and I like technology.
**✅ Why the model answer is stronger:** It focuses on relevant experience and measurable outcomes, showing professional maturity.
